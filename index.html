About Me

I finished my master degree in the Department of Computer Science, at the University of Colorado Boulder, advised by Danna Gurari.

Previously, I studied at the Nanjing University of Posts and Telecommunications, majoring in Information Security, advised by Lifa Wu.

I am open to full-time job now. If you're interested in my works or just want to chat, feel free to drop me an email! 🌟

My research interests mainly lie in computer vision, especially in:

  • Image Generation: Improving the controllability and fidelity of image generation with multimodal information.
  • Multimodal Learning: Aligning vision and language into cohesive multimodal representations.
